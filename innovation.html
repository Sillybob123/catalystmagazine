Explorer 1st Place — $1,500
HighEnroller

Contract-based research assistant staffing for clinical trial sites conducting industry-sponsored studies. By deploying pre-trained, GCP-certified research assistants on flexible contracts, HighEnroller enables Principal Investigators to convert fixed staffing costs into variable expenses tied to enrollment periods — alleviating financial burden during study gaps while ensuring immediate capacity during active trials.

A B2B marketplace transforming cafes into shoppable art galleries — through a website connecting artists with venues, View Point provides a subscription-based solution that streamlines curation, logistics, rotations, and insurance. CareCompass Health

Amid America's growing caregiving crisis, CareCompass Health helps hospitals reduce avoidable readmissions and caregiver burnout by embedding dementia-specific navigation into Epic. Combining clinical coordination with hands-on navigator support, the platform streamlines transitions, improves outcomes, strengthens equity, and provides families with consistent guidance.

About the NVC
GW's Competition
Overview

NVC brings together student founders from across GW to build, test, and pitch ventures with real market potential.

  • — Open to undergraduate, graduate, and professional students across all schools.
  • — Built around mentorship, iterative rounds, and public-facing pitch milestones.
  • — Designed to move ideas from concept stage to fundable business narratives.
