Writing & Editorial Guidelines

Story Structure

Every Catalyst story should follow professional journalism standards with a clear headline, compelling lede, nut graf explaining why it matters, supporting body content, and a strong conclusion.

Writing Standards

  • Accuracy – All facts, names, and quotes must be verified
  • Clarity – Use active voice; write for a general audience
  • Attribution – Cite all sources properly
  • Objectivity – Present multiple perspectives

Submission Checklist

  • ✓ Headline is specific and uses Title Case
  • ✓ Cover image is 16:9 and under 5MB
  • ✓ Category and tags are set correctly
  • ✓ No spelling or grammar errors
