About The Stacks

A monthly book recommendation, from one reader to another.

The Stacks is our monthly book column. Every month, a Catalyst writer picks a STEM book they actually liked and tells you about it: what the book is, what's good about it, and whether it's worth reading. The reviews are short and pretty casual. Think of them less as reviews and more as a friend recommending something they just finished.
