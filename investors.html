01 — Mission

Serious science writing, by the next generation.

Most student publications are class projects. We built ours like a real magazine — edited, designed, fact-checked — because that’s the only kind worth reading.

The Catalyst exists because the people best positioned to write about science — the students elbow-deep in the labs, the seminars, and the field work — almost never get a serious editorial home for it. Campus papers cover sports and tuition. National outlets cover discoveries after they’re old.

We sit in the gap. We pair student journalists with student researchers, run their drafts through real edits, and publish stories that actually move the conversation on campus and beyond.

Investing in The Catalyst isn’t just funding a magazine — it’s backing the next generation of science writers, editors, and product people in the city where so much of American science policy is shaped.

Six reasons The Catalyst is worth backing right now.

AI is rewriting how science gets explained — and who gets to explain it. The publications that figure out how to use these tools without losing their voice will define the next decade of science media. We’re building to be one of them.

03 — Revenue path

A sponsorship slot at the bottom of every story. Quiet, honest, lucrative.

Sponsored

Your brand. One thoughtful slot. End of every story.

Live

We’re building a single, well-designed sponsorship unit at the close of each article — the place every reader sees, none of them skip past, and no one mistakes for editorial. It’s the model that has worked for the best newsletters and serious magazines for a decade. It will work here.

  • One slot per story. No banners, no pop-ups, no programmatic clutter eroding the reading experience.
  • Curated partners only. Universities, research labs, student-services brands, and STEM employers — anyone we’d be proud to recommend.
  • Clearly labeled, clearly bounded. "Sponsored" sits above the slot. Coverage stays untouched. Trust stays intact.
  • Newsletter sponsorship, in parallel. One sponsor per send, same standards. The combined reach scales as we do.
