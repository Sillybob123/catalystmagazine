Faculty and Advisers

Eli Kintisch

Ted Turner Professor of Environmental Media

Guiding The Catalyst with a wealth of experience, Eli Kintisch serves as a key faculty adviser. As the Ted Turner Professor of Environmental Media at George Washington University, his expertise in climate journalism and environmental science provides invaluable mentorship to our student writers. Eli's distinguished career includes work as a contributing correspondent for Science magazine and creating impactful video series for PBS and Vox that explore critical environmental issues. His innovative approach to science communication—from developing augmented reality apps on sea-level rise to authoring the acclaimed book Hack the Planet—inspires our team to tackle complex topics with creativity and rigor. Eli’s guidance is instrumental in helping The Catalyst bridge the gap between academic research and compelling public storytelling.
